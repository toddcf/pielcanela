Services and Rates

Piel Canela Massage Therapy applies several massage modality techniques to each session to better serve every client’s requests.    To learn more about each modality offered, please visit the Massage Modalities page. If interested in booking a single specific modality, please consult with your therapist and specify when scheduling an appointment.

Piel Canela Massage Therapy does not work with healthcare providers. Rates are established according to the time of each session. Cash and all major credit cards are accepted.

Rates are subject to change without notice. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone.

Private Bookings                                                     

  • 30 minute massage $50 (outcall for group bookings only)
  • 60 minute massage $100
  • 90 minute massage $150

Prenatal Massage

  • 60 minute massage $130 

Multiple/Group Bookings

  • Office/Business                                                                      If interested in purchasing this service for employees, rates will be negotiated upon the number of interested employees. If two massage therapists are required, this is also an option. Bookings may be up to 4 hours.
  • Book a party                                                              Whatever the occasion, gather a few close friends and have a party. Each guest is responsible for their scheduled service. The host provides all party details and a private room with sufficient space for all services. The host receives 20% off their scheduled service. Therapist will provide all massage equipment, supplies and ambiance. Group bookings need to be scheduled at least a month in advance. Please review the cancellation policy with all guests. There is a 4-5 guest maximum depending on the services scheduled.

Refer A Friend                                                                        Refer a friend and receive 10% off of your next session. Valid after referral has purchased and redeemed their scheduled service. Non-transferable.

Book A Series                                                                              Purchase 5 massage sessions in advance and save $50! Sessions are non-transferable. Cancellation policy still applies. 

  • 60 minute massage series:   5 for $450 ($500 value)
  • 90 minute massage series:   5 for $700 ($750 value)
